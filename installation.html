The Visual Alphabet Installation allows users to type words or sentences into a system that generates a layered video. Users can explore words of their own personal motifs or enjoy creating visuals based on the diverse video elements. Every letter of the alphabet has been assigned a different visual and is layered in varied opacities to create the submitted words.

Users simply type their desired word and hit the spacebar on the device to generate the video. This installation is most effective when projected, but can also exist on a computer moniter.
